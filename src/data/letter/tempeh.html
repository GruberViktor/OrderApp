The Tempeh spores, unlike our Koji spores, don't need to be diluted, since they are mixed with rice flour already. If you are just starting out, I recommend to start with 2 g of tempeh starter per kg of cooked beans. Then, when you are getting better with tempeh, you can reduce the amount of starter to 1 g per kg.
